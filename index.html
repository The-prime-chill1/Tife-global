FAQ

How long does shipping take?
Air freight takes 5–7 working days from the UK to Nigeria. All parcels must arrive at our office before Wednesday for Thursday dispatch.
Do you offer insurance?
Yes, all cargo is fully insured. Additional coverage can be arranged for high-value items.
What is the minimum weight?
The minimum shipment weight is 10kg for door-to-door delivery.
Can I track my shipment?
Yes, use our tracking tool above with your unique tracking number.
Do you handle customs clearance?
Yes, we handle all customs documentation and clearance for both UK and Nigeria.
